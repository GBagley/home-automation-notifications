Program Name

Home Automation Notifications

Program Description

This SMS notification service is part of a personal home automation system. It sends automated alerts related to system status updates, environmental monitoring, and other home automation event notifications to the system owner.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on home automation events. Typical usage is fewer than 10 messages per day, though frequency may increase during unusual activity (e.g., repeated motion alerts).

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Support

For support, contact the system owner via GitHub: github.com/GBagley

You may also text HELP to receive support contact information.

Opt-Out

To stop receiving messages, text STOP at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
