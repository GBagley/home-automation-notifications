Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

This service is a personal home automation notification system operated by a single individual for private use.

Data Collected

The only personal data collected is the phone number of the system owner, entered manually into the system configuration.

How Data Is Used

The phone number is used solely to send home automation notifications such as system status updates and environmental alerts.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes. Your mobile phone number and any information collected as part of the SMS opt-in process will never be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Messages are transmitted via Twilio's SMS service solely for delivery purposes.
